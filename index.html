Meet The Owner

Arun Kumar

With over 15 years of experience in the art of barbering, Arun Kumar founded Fashion Hair Studio with a simple vision: to create a space where men can enjoy premium grooming services in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. His passion for precision and dedication to his craft are evident in every cut and shave.

Arun believes that a good haircut is more than just a routine—it's a boost of confidence. He personally trains his team to ensure the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.
